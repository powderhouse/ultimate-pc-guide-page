Why would I build a PC instead of buying a prebuilt?

There are many reasons people choose to build their own PC. Many people do it because you can actually save a lot of money, prebuilt PCs have all sorts of markups and build fees that can be avoided by building yourself. Another common reason is that you can get specialized hardware that isn't commonly available in prebuilts, like soundcards, capture cards, 10Gbps networking, and much more. Or maybe you just want a system that is unique in it's appearance and form factor.
