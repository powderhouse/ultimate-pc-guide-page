It's not the prettiest in the world, or the fastest, or the most upgradeable, but if your priority is to build the cheapest possible system with a decent GPU then this is a good base.

Do some research, I have specced this with an Intel Arc B580 GPU as it is the best overall budget option currently, but some games may not work as Intel GPUs are still a pretty new thing. I would advise that you do your own research and see if the games you play run better on the Intel Arc B580 or the Nvidia RTX 4060. Do not buy either GPU above MSRP, they are not worth it above their MSRPs.
