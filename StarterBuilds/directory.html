Starter Builds

These are various starter builds that you can either buy and put together as is, or use as a basis to modify to your liking, whether it be to fit a budget not listed or you just prefer a different case. I strongly encourage researching and editing so you can have a build that's really your's, that's one of the best part of custom PCs!

Intel Builds:

These builds all feature Intel-based processors, start with one of these if you do a mix of gaming and productivity tasks such as video editing, coding, or CAD.

Intel High-end Build

Good build for video editors, programmers, etc, with a large budget, but who are still conscious about getting the best bang for your buck.
